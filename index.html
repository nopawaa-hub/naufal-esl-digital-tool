Teaching Philosophy

I believe in Interactive Learning. Language isn't just memorized; it is experienced. My classroom approach prioritizes high student engagement through gamification, TPR (Total Physical Response), and low-anxiety environments where primary learners feel safe making mistakes and celebrating successes.

Purpose of this Portfolio

This platform centralizes my custom-built digital tools. Unlike static worksheets, these applications leverage AI, 3D graphics, and speech recognition to create practical classroom applications that teachers can project on a smartboard to instantly captivate their students.
